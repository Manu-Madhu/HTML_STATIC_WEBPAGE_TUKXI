About us


Auto Rickshaws connect millions of people all over India. But the whole process is a
time-consuming hassle─ finding a driver and haggling over prices. Our priority is to
innovate on the existing system to make it far more efficient and effective.

We know you’re tired of waiting and chasing Auto Rickshaws, we’re here to help. Our
Autos pick you up, we’ll even help you plan your trip in advance.When you request an

right to you.
